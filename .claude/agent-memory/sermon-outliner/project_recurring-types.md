Running log of types/themes the preacher revisits and the Message sources that proved strongest, so future outlines can reach for them quickly.

**Why:** Reuse of validated quotes saves research time and keeps typology consistent across the congregation's sermon series.

**How to apply:** Check here first when a theme recurs; still re-run searches to confirm the quote and paragraph number before using.

- **Babylon = confusion / called-out Bride:** strong sources — 1953-11-10 "I Will Restore" ¶9 (Babylon traced Genesis->middle OT->Revelation); 1960-12-25 "God's Wrapped Gift" ¶87 ("Come out of Babylon, confusion"). Used in 2026-06-03 "Is it God's plan?".
- **Jeremiah 29 / "expected end" / captivity-with-a-promise:** Branham's "Desperations" (1963-09-01) reads Jer 29:10-14 directly — ¶31-32 key. The definitive source for this whole theme.
- **False prophets / seducing spirits / "try the spirits" (last days):** 1961-12-31 "You Must Be Born Again" ¶88 (false prophets keep enough true Word to be false); 1960-03-08 "Discernment Of Spirit" ¶24 (1 John 4:1, motive test); 1963-03-19 "Seals 4" ¶45 (seducing clergy spirit).
- **"Stay with the Word" (true prophet mark):** 1961-10-01 "It Becometh Us To Fulfil All Righteousness" ¶57; 1962-04-08 "Presuming" ¶71.
- **Falling away / man of sin (2 Thess 2:3):** 1963-06-30 "Is Your Life Worthy Of The Gospel?" ¶41.
- **Serpent beguiled Eve = deception / serpent seed:** 1958-09-28 "The Serpent's Seed" ¶71 ("beguile" means "defiled", not an apple — the definitive line); 1963-04-21 "Victory Day" ¶33 (Eve lost the focus, "two ways" vs "God said so, that settles it"); 1962-07-13 "From that time" ¶55 (death reigned from when Eve reasoned). Used in 2026-06-16 "One Word Off — The Lie That Beguiles".
- **White horse = antichrist spirit (1st seal), NOT Holy Spirit:** 1963-03-18 "Seals 3: The First Seal" ¶119 (refutes the Cupid/Holy-Spirit teaching); 1963-03-20 "Seals 5: The Third Seal" ¶104 (white horse innocent teacher = Nicolaitane/antichrist, ties straight to Satan beguiling Eve, "slick bird"). The bridge text linking serpent-in-garden to the church-age deception.
- **Stay with the Word / can't deceive the elect (last-days antichrist):** 1964-08-23 "Questions and Answers, Part 2" ¶5 (pastoral: "stay with that Word... antichrist can't get the whole Word together"); 1956-04-29 "Jehovah-Jireh" ¶59 (stay with the Bible, sheep know His voice); 1961-04-11 "But it wasn't so from the beginning" ¶28 (elect cannot be deceived, names in the book).
- **Flattery as the antichrist's weapon (answers "flattery or enchantment?"):** Daniel 11:21 "he shall come in peaceably, and obtain the kingdom by flatteries" + Daniel 11:32 "such as do wickedly... shall he corrupt by flatteries: but the people that do know their God shall be strong." Sources — 1963-03-18 "Seals 3: First Seal" ¶137 (Branham reads Dan 11:21 of antichrist taking the church kingdoms by flattery); 1957-09-15 "Hebrews Ch.7 pt1" ¶19 (Dan 11:32 quoted, "people that know their God shall be strong"); 1961-01-29 "Turning Northward" ¶49 ("it will be the flatteries that it's taken"); 1962-04-22 "Restoration of the Bride Tree" ¶156 ("come in with flatteries like a lamb... behind it it's a wolf"). The Scriptural bridge from a personal scam to the white-horse rider. Used in 2026-06-16 serpent-seed v2.
- **Snake charms the bird / enchantment (look up to be free):** 1958-10-03 "Looking at the unseen" ¶32 (snake charms bird with enchantments/stare of eyes; bird flutters, paralyzed, swallowed — antidote: look upward); 1959-04-10 "Looking at the unseen" ¶34 (same, "flutter your wings and look upward quickly and see Jesus"). Pairs with the flattery/enchantment theme.
- **Genesis 3:15 enmity / Redeemer (love funnel, Eden->Golgotha):** 1953-08-30 "Why I am a holy-roller" ¶38 (bloody sheepskin traced from Eden to Golgotha — best altar-call close); 1953-11-11 "Preparation" ¶26 (space funnels to L-O-V-E, "promising a redeemer").
