**1** Have a few Scriptures written down that I would like to speak to you from, and I trust that God will bless our feeble efforts. Now, many people has wondered why we are so odd and so noisy. You know, this is kind of a different type of a convention than what people are used to seeing; and usually everything is kind of a cut and dried form. That's the only thing that we know to do. And God does the rest of it. So that makes us a very peculiar acting people. The other day someone said, "You know, you people are really an oddball." **3** One day in California, I was going down the streets of Los Angeles, and I seen a man had a sign on his breast and here; and it said, "I am a fool for Christ."
