**1** This meeting is to be a little different meeting than what we usually have here. Mostly every time when we come together here, it's a meeting for the healing of the sick and for the physical needs. The emphasis is put upon that. **2** Now, we know that when a body is healed, that makes us all happy, because we know that it definitely shows that our God heals the sick.
