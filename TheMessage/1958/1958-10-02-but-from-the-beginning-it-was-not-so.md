# But from the beginning it was not so



**1** Good evening, friends. Very happy to be back tonight in the service of the Lord God, and trusting His blessings upon the services tonight. I was glad to see tonight Brother Robert Daugherty back in the room there. It was Brother Daugherty that sponsored my first campaign. And I remember going to him ... there's still some people sitting here that helped me get over there; and Brother Creech here, I think is one of them.

**2** The visions had just begin to happen. And the Angel of the Lord had just met me up here at Green's Mill; told me what was going to take place. And it wasn't hardly two weeks till he called for me to come over to St. Louis. His girl was with some kind of ... like St. Vitas Dance. And we went there and prayed, and made all kind of vows, and it looked like nothing was just happening. I was just going to stay because I didn't have campaigns before that. Just till it was over that I found out what the Lord would do.

**3** You know Brother Robert, I believe it'd been better if I'd just kept it that way. Course, there'd probably wouldn't been as many saved, but just stay with one case till it's over; till you know. The next day, I guess it was along two or three o'clock in the afternoon ... or, that same day, I don't remember now, I was sitting in a car outside of the parsonage and his father was there and I was by myself. I felt the Spirit of the Lord come and I went outside the parsonage and sat out there. And little Betty was just like a wild person, just screaming; and doctors could do nothing for her. And mother and dad, both, they looked very bad; they'd sat up days and weeks with her. And there a vision came just before the car, in front of the car. **4** I suppose you said she'd be here tomorrow night (Didn't you, Brother Robert?) to testify. We would ask especially tonight too, Lord, for those who are sick, needy, and afflicted. Thy great healing power and mercy will rest upon each of them. Bless Thy Word going forth, Lord.
