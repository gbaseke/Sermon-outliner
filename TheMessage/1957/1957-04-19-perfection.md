He had to prosecute the sinner, because he had overstepped the boundaries of grace, and had become a law-breaking citizen. Then, he must be dealt with. And every law has a penalty. For, the penalty of transgressing God's law is death. And it had to project death to the human race. And all of the human race is under the penalty of this law.
