# Interruption of Nature — Rev 16:2-4

Search results from the Message related to the vials poured on the earth, sea, and rivers (Rev 16:2-4) and Brother Branham's teaching on the interruption of nature.

---

## Core Teaching — Sixth Seal as Interruption of Nature

**1963-03-23 — Seals 8: The Sixth Seal — ¶41**
> "Then all nature was interrupted... The earthquakes, the sun went black, and the moon wouldn't give its light, and the stars shook and fell."

**1963-03-23 — Seals 8: The Sixth Seal — ¶103**
> "They can do nature any way they want to... It's the power of God to interrupt nature. See, the sixth seal is completely an interruption..."

**1963-03-23 — Seals 8: The Sixth Seal — ¶107**
> "What did Moses do? He turned the waters into blood, he done all these kinds of miracles, just exactly what's predicted here under this sixth seal. And here they are in Revelation 11, doing the very same thing!" — He ties the two witnesses (Rev 11), Moses's plagues, and the sixth seal together.

**1963-04-28 — Look — ¶16**
> "In this sixth seal there is a great interruption in nature. The moon, the stars, the earth belches forth. The church is purged, Israel is purged, and everything is purged under that seventh seal so that the millennium can start in."

## Key Pattern

Brother Branham links Rev 16:2-4 (vials on earth/sea/rivers turning to blood) directly to the **sixth seal** and to the **two witnesses of Rev 11**, seeing them as the same event — Moses-type judgment, an interruption of the natural order by the Word of God.
