# Second Vial — The Sea Becomes Blood
## Revelation 16:3

---

### Scripture (KJV)
> **Revelation 16:3** — And the second angel poured out his vial upon the sea; and it became as the blood of a dead man: and every living soul died in the sea.

### Cross-Reference — First Plague of Egypt (KJV)
> **Exodus 7:20-21** — And Moses and Aaron did so, as the LORD commanded; and he lifted up the rod, and smote the waters that were in the river, in the sight of Pharaoh, and in the sight of his servants; and all the waters that were in the river were turned to blood. And the fish that was in the river died; and the river stank, and the Egyptians could not drink of the water of the river; and there was blood throughout all the land of Egypt.

### Cross-Reference — Second Trumpet (KJV)
> **Revelation 8:8-9** — And the second angel sounded, and as it were a great mountain burning with fire was cast into the sea: and the third part of the sea became blood; And the third part of the creatures which were in the sea, and had life, died; and the third part of the ships were destroyed.

### Cross-Reference — The Meaning of "Sea" (KJV)
> **Revelation 17:15** — And he saith unto me, The waters which thou sawest, where the whore sitteth, are peoples, and multitudes, and nations, and tongues.

---

## 1. What Does "The Sea" Mean?

In Revelation, **the sea** carries two layers of meaning — literal and symbolic. Branham consistently taught from the symbolic dimension of prophetic language. Revelation 17:15 makes the symbolism explicit: the waters where the great whore sits are **peoples, multitudes, nations, and tongues**.

When the second vial is poured upon the sea and it becomes as the blood of a dead man, this speaks of a vast judgment upon the restless mass of unregenerate humanity — the nations and peoples who have followed the beast and lived under the influence of the harlot church system.

Branham tied this symbol of "sea as peoples" directly to his teaching on the great whore:

> "And there came one of the seven angels which had the seven vials, and talked with me, saying, Come hither; and I will show thee the judgment of the great whore that sitteth upon many waters."
> — *Revelation 17:1 (KJV)*

> "Now, anyone knows that when a woman is symbolized in the Bible it pertains to a church… 'The great whore that sits upon many waters.' Now, 'woman sitting upon many waters' sounds very mystic, but it's all here. The Bible explains it."
> — *1960-09-11, Five Definite Identifications of the True Church, ¶27*

The sea upon which the apostate church has sat — the vast multitudes of people it has deceived and controlled — now becomes blood.

---

## 2. Blood of a Dead Man

The text specifies not just blood, but **"the blood of a dead man"** — a particularly vivid and repulsive image. The blood of a living person still carries life. But the blood of a dead man is putrid, coagulated, stinking, and utterly useless.

This is the condition of the apostate religious sea — the life of the Spirit has long departed. What remains is the form of religion without the power. The doctrines are dead. The worship is dead. The Word has been replaced with creeds, catechisms, and traditions. The spiritual corruption is already present; the second vial simply reveals and ratifies what is already true.

The contrast is with the **living water** that Christ offers (John 4:10; John 7:38) — and with the **pure river of water of life** that flows from the throne in Revelation 22:1. The sea of apostate humanity, having rejected the living water, becomes dead blood.

---

## 3. Every Living Soul Died in the Sea

**Every living soul died in the sea** — the totality of the judgment upon the apostate masses is expressed here. There is no exception for those whose hope rested in the beast's system. This is the full outpouring of what Revelation 14:10 announced — "the wine of the wrath of God, poured out without mixture."

Compare this to the Second Trumpet (Rev 8:8-9), where only a **third part** of the sea became blood and a third part of living creatures died. The trumpet was partial — a warning shot. The vial is **total** — complete, final judgment. The progression from trumpets to vials is a progression from warning to execution.

---

## 4. Parallel — First Plague of Egypt

| Egypt (Type) | Vial (Antitype) |
|---|---|
| Waters of Egypt turned to blood (Ex 7:20) | Sea becomes blood of a dead man (Rev 16:3) |
| Fish in the river died; river stank (Ex 7:21) | Every living soul died in the sea |
| Egyptians could not drink | No life remains in the apostate sea |
| Magicians replicated the sign (Ex 7:22) | False church cannot produce life |
| Israel in Goshen had clean water | Bride already raptured, untouched |

Branham taught that the plagues of Egypt all repeat in the end time:

> "All of it repeats at least once."
> — *1958-03-09, The Handwriting on the Wall, ¶7*

The first plague in Egypt was water turned to blood — judgment upon the life-source of Egypt. The Nile was Egypt's entire economy, food supply, and civilization. To turn it to blood was to strike at the very foundation. In the same way, the second vial strikes at the vast sea of apostate humanity — the foundation of the beast's power base.

---

## 5. Contrast With Second Trumpet

The **Second Trumpet** (Rev 8:8-9) is God's partial judgment upon the sea — a warning during the tribulation period specifically directed toward Israel (as Branham taught, the trumpets pertain to Israel):

> "I want you to know, friends, that striking on these things, I'm certainly leaving out weeks of teaching; but just kind of hitting it so that when the weather cools off, or a little later, when we get into those seven seals, seven vials, seven trumpets, all these things, that I can refer back."
> — *1961-07-30, Daniel 9: Gabriel's Instructions to Daniel, ¶46*

The vials fall on the Gentile, apostate world system. The progression is clear:

- **Trumpet 2**: 1/3 of sea = blood, 1/3 of creatures die (partial warning)
- **Vial 2**: Entire sea = blood of a dead man, all living souls die (total judgment)

---

## 6. Timing and the Hardening of Hearts

Like the plagues of Egypt that hardened Pharaoh's heart rather than bringing repentance, the vials produce the same response — **blasphemy rather than repentance** (see Rev 16:9, 11, 21). The beast-worshipping world does not cry out for mercy when the second vial falls. Its capacity for repentance has been exhausted by persistent rejection of the gospel.

This is the terrible irony of the vials: they make plain what the world has chosen. Having rejected the **water of life**, the world receives blood. Having rejected the **Prince of Life**, every living soul in the sea of the apostate masses dies.

The Bride is untouched — already caught away:

> "The church will not go through the tribulation! Do you see? They was already in glory."
> — *1961-01-08, Revelation Chapter 4c: Throne of Mercy and Judgement, ¶85*
