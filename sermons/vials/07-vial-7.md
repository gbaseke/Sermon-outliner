## Revelation 16:17-21

---

## 1. Poured Into the Air — Satan's Domain

The seventh vial is unique in its target: **the air**. The first five vials struck the earth (land, sea, rivers, sun, throne of beast). The sixth prepared the way for Armageddon. The seventh is poured into the **air** — the unseen realm above the earth.

This is profoundly significant. Paul identifies Satan as **"the prince of the power of the air"** (Ephesians 2:2) — the ruling spiritual power in the atmospheric heavens, the domain from which he exercises his authority over "the children of disobedience." The seventh vial strikes Satan's own domain.

> **Ephesians 2:2** — Wherein in time past ye walked according to the course of this world, according to the prince of the power of the air, the spirit that now worketh in the children of disobedience. *(KJV)*

This is the ultimate confrontation: God's wrath invading Satan's throne-room in the heavens. Every other vial has struck things below (earth, sea, rivers) or things above used by men (sun) or the beast's earthly seat. The seventh strikes the very atmosphere where the spirit of this age dwells. It is the divine invasion of the enemy's headquarters.

---

## 2. "It Is Done" — The Voice from the Throne

> "There came a great voice out of the temple of heaven, from the throne, saying, **It is done.**"
> — *Revelation 16:17 (KJV)*

This declaration from the throne of heaven — **"It is done"** — is the most final statement in human history since Christ's cry on the cross: **"It is finished"** (John 19:30). The parallel is unmistakable and deliberate:

| Calvary | Seventh Vial |
|---|---|
| "It is finished" — redemption complete | "It is done" — judgment complete |
| Spoken by Christ as He gave up His spirit | Spoken from the throne of heaven |
| Sealed the new covenant in blood | Seals the final judgment in wrath |
| Opened the way to God for believers | Closes the door on the apostate world |
| Followed by earthquake (Matt 27:51) | Followed by greatest earthquake in history |

At Calvary, **Christ finished the work of redemption**. At the seventh vial, **God finishes the work of judgment**. The two great "finished" moments of all history — one of mercy, one of wrath.

Branham taught that mercy and judgment are both essential attributes of God:

> "In order to be love, He has to have judgment, to be just. So, He is a merciful God tonight. But, my friend, when you stand before Him at judgment on that dark, cloudy day, He'll be a God full of anger."
> — *1961-02-17, The Seal of God and the Mark of the Beast Part 2, ¶40*

"It is done" is the moment when every offer of mercy is conclusively withdrawn. The day of grace for the beast system is irrevocably closed.

## 3. Voices, Thunders, Lightnings

As with the seventh seal (Rev 8:5), the seventh trumpet (Rev 11:19), and the seventh vial — **voices, thunders, and lightnings** accompany the final events. These are the signs of the immediate, manifest presence and power of God. Throughout Revelation, this triple manifestation marks the decisive moments of divine action.

> "There were voices, and thunders, and lightnings."
> — *Revelation 16:18 (KJV)*

Branham spoke of the seven thunders and their connection to the final events:

> "Last week's tapes, I think, will reveal to you the great hideous things, that we're going to speak on one of these days when we can get a place sufficient, of the opening of those last plagues to be poured out upon the earth… those vials, rather, pouring out of the vials, and the seven thunders…"
> — *1965-08-22, Christ is Revealed in His Own Word, ¶15*

## 4. The Great Earthquake

> "There was a great earthquake, such as was not since men were upon the earth, so mighty an earthquake, and so great."
> — *Revelation 16:18 (KJV)*

This earthquake surpasses every previous earthquake in all of human history. Its magnitude is unparalleled. It is the physical shaking that corresponds to the complete collapse of the world-system under God's final judgment.

### Connection to the Sixth Seal

The sixth seal (Rev 6:12) opened with a great earthquake, and the sun became black as sackcloth, the moon became as blood, and every mountain and island were moved. Branham taught the sixth seal as Israel's sign — the tribulation events that call Israel to national repentance. The seventh vial's earthquake is related but distinct — it is the final culmination of all the earth-shaking events of the tribulation period.

> "And I beheld when he had opened the sixth seal, and, lo, there was a great earthquake; and the sun became black as sackcloth of hair."
> — *1963-03-23, Seals 8: The Sixth Seal, ¶36*

### The Los Angeles Prophecy

Branham prophesied a catastrophic earthquake that would sink the city of Los Angeles into the ocean. He connected this to the end-time judgment upon America:

> "And then prophesying, 'Los Angeles will be in the bottom of the sea,' and I come home and went to Africa; and while I was in Africa, they had a earthquake, and scientists… seen it; it was on a broadcast that some of the big, fine homes tumbled in (in Los Angeles), and a motel, and so forth. And now, there is… since that earthquake, there's a two or three inch crack that come in the earth, starting in Alaska, goes around through the Aleutian Islands."
> — *1965-07-11, Ashamed, ¶22*

The greatest earthquake that has ever shaken the earth since men were upon it — the seventh vial's earthquake — will complete what the prophetic warning earthquakes have already begun to signal.

### Every Island and Mountain Fled

> "Every island fled away, and the mountains were not found."
> — *Revelation 16:20 (KJV)*

This echoes the sixth seal: "every mountain and island were moved out of their places" (Rev 6:14). But the seventh vial language is more absolute — mountains were **not found**. The geological upheaval is so total that the very landscape of the earth is transformed.

This also connects to Zechariah 14, where the Mount of Olives splits at the return of Christ, and the whole land of Israel is remade.

## 5. Great Babylon Comes in Remembrance

> "Great Babylon came in remembrance before God, to give unto her the cup of the wine of the fierceness of his wrath."
> — *Revelation 16:19 (KJV)*

The word **"remembrance"** here is judicial — God has not forgotten Babylon's crimes. Every persecution, every martyrdom, every corrupted soul, every false doctrine, every drop of martyr's blood is on the divine ledger. The seventh vial is the moment God settles the account.

The cup given to Babylon is the **cup of the wine of the fierceness of his wrath** — the same cup she forced others to drink (Rev 17:4, 18:6 — "double unto her double"). This is the great judgment that Revelation 17-18 elaborates in detail: the fall of Mystery Babylon, the mourning of the kings and merchants, the rejoicing of heaven.

Branham identified Babylon as Rome throughout:

> "He hath judged the great whore, which did corrupt the earth with her fornication, and has avenged the blood of His servants at her hand. [That's the ending of the Catholic church.]"
> — *1958-09-27, Why Are We Not a Denomination?, ¶75*

## 6. Hailstones of a Talent Weight

> "There fell upon men a great hail out of heaven, every stone about the weight of a talent."
> — *Revelation 16:21 (KJV)*

A talent weighs approximately 75-100 pounds. These are not hailstones — they are boulders of ice falling from the sky. This parallels the **seventh plague of Egypt** — great hail and fire mingled (Exodus 9:23-24), "such as there was none like it in all the land of Egypt since it became a nation."

Yet even under these apocalyptic hailstones, **men blasphemed God** rather than repenting. The final response of an unrepentant world to the fiercest judgment ever poured upon the earth is the same as its response to every previous vial — **blasphemy**.

This is the most chilling statement in all of Revelation. Not one voice cries out in repentance. The hearts of men under the seventh vial are fully and finally hardened beyond recovery. The sentence of the vials is completely just.

## 7. Connection to the Seventh Trumpet

The **Seventh Trumpet** (Rev 11:15-19) announced the completion of the mystery of God and the beginning of Christ's reign. The Seventh Vial closes that proclamation with the execution of final judgment:

| Seventh Trumpet | Seventh Vial |
|---|---|
| "The kingdoms of this world are become… of our Lord" (Rev 11:15) | The kingdoms fall as Babylon is destroyed |
| Temple opened in heaven; ark seen (Rev 11:19) | Great voice from the temple: "It is done" |
| Lightnings, voices, thunderings, earthquake, great hail (Rev 11:19) | Same phenomena, amplified to maximum intensity |

The seventh trumpet announces what the seventh vial executes.

---

## 8. What Comes After — Revelation 17-19

The seventh vial does not end the story — it introduces the detailed expansion of Babylon's fall in Revelation 17-19. The one-sentence judgment of "great Babylon came in remembrance" (Rev 16:19) is unpacked in full detail:

- **Revelation 17** — The identity of the great whore (Mystery Babylon), her judgement by the ten kings
- **Revelation 18** — The fall of commercial and religious Babylon; mourning of merchants and kings; rejoicing of heaven
- **Revelation 19** — The marriage supper of the Lamb; the return of Christ in power; the battle of Armageddon; the beast and false prophet cast into the lake of fire

The seventh vial is the hinge — it closes the vials and opens the final chapters of the age.

---

## 9. Summary — The End of All Things

The seventh vial brings:
- The air — Satan's domain — struck with divine wrath
- The voice of God from His throne: "It is done"
- The greatest earthquake in all of human history
- The destruction of Mystery Babylon
- The dissolution of the world's geography
- Hailstones of 75-100 pounds killing without mercy
- Final, irrevocable blasphemy from the hardened world

And then — silence. The vials are finished. The wrath of God is complete. Christ returns. The Millennial Reign begins.

> "It'll finally wind up into the great battle of Armageddon. That'll be the last of it. When she comes… Looky here, they stood on the sea of glass and sung the song of Moses."
> — *1963-04-21, Victory Day, ¶50, 56*
