# Fifth Vial — Darkness on the Throne of the Beast
## Revelation 16:10-11

---

### Scripture (KJV)
> **Revelation 16:10** — And the fifth angel poured out his vial upon the seat of the beast; and his kingdom was full of darkness; and they gnawed their tongues for pain,
>
> **Revelation 16:11** — And blasphemed the God of heaven because of their pains and their sores, and repented not of their deeds.

### Cross-Reference — Ninth Plague of Egypt (KJV)
> **Exodus 10:21-23** — And the LORD said unto Moses, Stretch out thine hand toward heaven, that there may be darkness over the land of Egypt, even darkness which may be felt. And Moses stretched forth his hand toward heaven; and there was a thick darkness in all the land of Egypt three days: They saw not one another, neither rose any man from his place for three days: but all the children of Israel had light in their dwellings.

### Cross-Reference — Fifth Trumpet (KJV)
> **Revelation 9:1-2** — And the fifth angel sounded, and I saw a star fall from heaven unto the earth: and to him was given the key of the bottomless pit. And he opened the bottomless pit; and there arose a smoke out of the pit, as the smoke of a great furnace; and the sun and the air were darkened by reason of the smoke of the pit.

---

## 1. The Target — The Seat of the Beast

Unlike the previous vials that struck broad targets (earth, sea, rivers, sun), the fifth vial is aimed with surgical precision at **the seat (throne) of the beast**. This is the epicenter of the beast's authority and power.

Branham identified this seat without hesitation:

> "That's where it began right there, in Rome, and that's where the seat of the beast is, and from there come the old mother whore."
> — *1964-08-23, Questions and Answers Part 1, ¶162*

**The seat of the beast is Rome** — the Vatican, the papal throne. The fifth vial strikes the very headquarters of the apostate religious system that has deceived the nations for centuries. The entire kingdom of the beast — its satellites, its daughter denominations, its political alliances — is plunged into darkness.

---

## 2. His Kingdom Was Full of Darkness

**Darkness** in Scripture is the antithesis of God's presence. God is light; in Him is no darkness at all (1 John 1:5). When God's presence departs completely, darkness fills the void.

This darkness is not merely physical obscurity — it is the **complete withdrawal of divine light, truth, and grace** from the beast's kingdom. The false church has been in spiritual darkness throughout the church ages — preaching a form of godliness but denying the power. Now that spiritual darkness is ratified and made absolute by divine decree.

Branham taught repeatedly that the Roman church and her daughters had rejected the Light of God's Word:

> "She's teaching something that isn't true. That's the Nicolaitanes. See it coming here? Going about putting people under the yoke of an organization."
> — *1960-12-07, Church Ages Part 5: The Pergamean Church Age, ¶92*

> "The great whore of Revelation 17… she's the mother of harlots also."
> — *1965-07-25, The Anointed Ones at the End Time, ¶178*

The kingdom that placed itself in the seat of God's authority — claiming to be the kingdom of light — is now revealed as the kingdom of darkness. The darkness is both judgment and revelation: God is pulling back the curtain and showing what the beast's kingdom truly is.

---

## 3. They Gnawed Their Tongues for Pain

The physical anguish described — **gnawing their tongues for pain** — suggests an anguish so intense that sufferers cannot control themselves. The pain accumulates and intensifies. Note that verse 11 mentions "their pains and their sores" (plural), linking back to the **first vial** (the grievous sores). The plagues are cumulative — each new vial adds to the suffering of the previous ones without providing any relief.

This is the judicial nature of the vials: no relief is provided between judgments. There is no divine respite, no window of mercy. The day of grace is fully and finally closed.

---

## 4. Egypt's Ninth Plague — Darkness That Could Be Felt

The parallel in Egypt is the **ninth plague** — "darkness which may be felt" (Exodus 10:21). It was not merely dim light, but a palpable, heavy, oppressive darkness that paralyzed the Egyptians for three days. Yet in Goshen, where Israel dwelt, every person had light in his dwelling.

The ninth plague in Egypt was immediately before the tenth plague — the death of the firstborn. In the same way, the fifth vial of darkness immediately precedes the sixth vial (Euphrates and the gathering to Armageddon) and the seventh vial (the final "It is done"). The darkness is the penultimate judgment before the end.

| Egypt (Type) | Vial (Antitype) |
|---|---|
| Thick darkness over Egypt three days (Ex 10:22) | Kingdom of beast full of darkness (Rev 16:10) |
| "Darkness which may be felt" — oppressive | Men gnaw tongues for pain in the darkness |
| Egyptians could not see one another | Total spiritual and physical blindness |
| Israel had light in their dwellings (Ex 10:23) | Bride already in glory, in God's Light |
| Preceded tenth plague (death of firstborn) | Precedes sixth vial (Armageddon) |

---

## 5. "Blasphemed the God of Heaven… and Repented Not"

The response to the fifth vial is again **blasphemy** rather than repentance. Even in total darkness, even gnawing their tongues for pain, even knowing God has power over these plagues — the beast-worshipping world does not repent.

This demonstrates the finality of their choice. They have crossed a line that cannot be uncrossed. Their wills have been permanently set against God. This is the ultimate spiritual darkness — not merely an absence of light, but an active rejection of light even when the consequences are utterly devastating.

Branham saw this hardening in his generation:

> "Men now and people today are in such a neurotic condition, the whole world…"
> — *1965-08-22, Christ is Revealed in His Own Word, ¶15*

The neurosis of the tribulation world — gnawing tongues, blaspheming God — is the culmination of the spiritual insanity that already characterizes those who have rejected truth.

---

## 6. Connection to Fifth Trumpet

The **Fifth Trumpet** (Rev 9:1-11) released locusts from the bottomless pit — demonic, tormenting spirits that had the power to torment men for five months. The locusts brought anguish that made men "seek death, and shall not find it; and shall desire to die, and death shall flee from them" (Rev 9:6). Smoke from the pit darkened the sun and the air.

The Fifth Vial continues this theme of tormenting darkness and anguish, now concentrated and amplified at its source — the throne of the beast. If the trumpet released demonic torment upon the general population, the vial zeros in on the headquarters of the beast's power.

---

## 7. Timing — The Seat Struck at the Height of Its Power

The beast's "seat" implies the beast is fully enthroned and operating at the height of his power when this vial falls. This is during the middle or latter portion of the tribulation — after the peace covenant has been broken, after the abomination of desolation, after the beast has demanded universal worship. At the very moment of his greatest apparent triumph, God strikes his throne with darkness.

This is the pattern of God's judgment throughout Scripture — He allows the enemy to reach the height of pride and apparent victory before the decisive stroke falls:

> "God doesn't call man to judgment without warning."
> — *1963-07-24, God Doesn't Call Man to Judgement Without Warning, ¶56*

The five preceding vials have all been warnings leading to this moment. Now God strikes the throne itself.
