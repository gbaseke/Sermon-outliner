## 1. The Euphrates — River of Babylon, Boundary of Empires

The **Euphrates River** carries enormous prophetic weight throughout Scripture. It is the great river of Babylon — the very river that ran through the ancient city, around which the empire was built:

> "The Euphrates River has a great place in the Bible. The first place, we find that it run right through Eden, the Euphrates River did… The next thing we find out, the Euphrates river also was the river that come right straight down through Babylon."
> — *1961-08-08, Thy House, ¶75*

Historically, Babylon fell to Cyrus the Great when Persian armies diverted the Euphrates and marched under the city's walls through the dry riverbed. The drying of the Euphrates thus carries a prophetic echo: **as ancient Babylon fell when the river was dried, so Mystery Babylon falls when the sixth vial dries the Euphrates**.

> "See, the Euphrates River runs today… The Euphrates River is when this great… made way for it to come down, see, making way for the king of the north to come down, the kings. That'll take place during the time of the battle of Armageddon in the last days."
> — *1961-08-08, Thy House, ¶76*

The drying of the Euphrates **prepares the way for the kings of the east** — a massive gathering of nations toward the Middle East for the final confrontation. Simultaneously, the three frog-spirits go out to gather the entire world.

## 2. Three Unclean Spirits Like Frogs — The Antichrist Trinity

This is one of the most significant prophetic revelations in the sixth vial — and one that Branham expounded with unique clarity. **Three unclean spirits like frogs** emerge from:

1. The mouth of **the dragon**
2. The mouth of **the beast**
3. The mouth of **the false prophet**

Branham identified these three spirits as the **unholy trinity** — the spiritual deception of trinitarianism that originated in the Nicaea Council:

> "Notice here something real quick now while we're ending this our talk. Notice Revelation 16:13-14. If you want to put that down… Now notice, this is the sounding between the Sixth and Seventh Vial. Now, we'll close out just in a minute, if you can just bear a few minutes longer. Closing out now, notice. Revelation 16:13-14, between the sixth and seventh vial, three unclean spirits like frogs (did you notice that?) went out of the mouth of the dragon, and out of the mouth of the beast, and out of the mouth of the false prophet."
> — *1965-07-25, The Anointed Ones at the End Time, ¶170*

> "Started where? This is false prophet (singular). False prophet, the first pope. And from there come up the whore and the mother of harlots, the whole thing. A false trinity was rising, not in the early days, wouldn't be made manifest in the early days, it went right on through with it. But when the seven seals become and opened those mysteries and revealed them, that's when the frogs, three unclean spirits like frogs, come out to manifest themselves: **a trinity doctrine against the truth**."
> — *1965-07-25, The Anointed Ones at the End Time, ¶174*

### The Frog — Always Looking Backward

Branham emphasized the significance of the **frog** as the symbol chosen for these spirits:

> "A trinity of frogs. A frog is an animal that always looks backward. He never looks where he's going; he looks where he's come from, see. Don't you see? Where was trinitarianism born at? Remember, three unclean spirits, individual spirits. Are you getting it? Notice. They look back to the Nicaea Council where the trinity doctrine was born at — not in the Bible; there's no such a thing. They look back to the Nicaea Council at Nicaea, Rome, where the trinity was born at. Notice where they come from."
> — *1965-07-25, The Anointed Ones at the End Time, ¶172*

A frog never looks forward; it always looks back. The false trinity spirits are identified by their backward orientation — looking back to Nicaea (325 AD) and to Roman church councils rather than forward to the pure Word of God.

### Earlier Identification — Three Isms

In an earlier teaching, Branham connected the three frog-spirits to the three great political "isms" of the 20th century:

> "There'll be the three unclean spirits that'll go forth. I really, truly, believe right now… But it's three spirits, just like communism, fascism, and we brought that out years ago, you know. Remember, I told you, I said it would be… a thing would take place, it would all wind up in communism."
> — *1961-08-08, Thy House, ¶74*

> "The three isms. Let me type it for you now, then you'll see where it started, where it began, see. There was communism, fascism, Nazism. They were spirits. Communism is a spirit. It gets on you, see. It's a spirit."
> — *1961-08-08, Thy House, ¶73*

By 1965, Branham had refined this identification — connecting the three frog spirits specifically to the trinitarian religious deception rather than to political systems alone. The political and religious dimensions are both expressions of the same underlying spirit of antichrist.

### What a Frog Represents

> "Unclean spirits… they're like frogs. Did you ever notice how a frog looks? A frog is always looking backwards. He never looks forward; looking backward."
> — *1954-05-15, Questions and Answers, ¶31*

The frog-spirit is characterized by:
- **Backward orientation** — tradition over Scripture, councils over the Word
- **Uncleanness** — under the Mosaic law frogs were unclean animals (Lev 11:10-12)
- **Pervasiveness** — like the frogs of Egypt that were everywhere, in every room, under every sheet

## 3. Working Miracles to Deceive

> "For they are the spirits of devils, working miracles, which go forth unto the kings of the earth and of the whole world, to gather them to the battle of that great day of God Almighty."
> — *Revelation 16:14 (KJV)*

The three frog-spirits are not impotent — they **work miracles**. This is the final satanic deception — counterfeit signs and wonders gathering the nations. Branham warned throughout his ministry about the end-time deception of false miracles:

> "When we open up those, the Lord willing, on those seven vials and show those hideous things… Men will be so insane after while, till they'll imagine they're seeing ants the size of a mountain. They'll be tormenting women, be locusts come upon the earth with long hair to torment women who cut theirs off."
> — *1965-08-22, Christ is Revealed in His Own Word, ¶18*

The miracle-working spirits gather the **kings of the earth** — political and military leaders — toward one final battle. The entire world system, blinded by demonic deception and driven by the false trinity spirits, marches toward its own destruction.

## 4. "Behold, I Come as a Thief" — A Warning in the Middle of Judgment

Remarkably, **verse 15 interrupts the narrative of gathering armies** with a direct word from Christ:

> "Behold, I come as a thief. Blessed is he that watcheth, and keepeth his garments, lest he walk naked, and they see his shame."
> — *Revelation 16:15 (KJV)*

This insertion — in the middle of the sixth vial, between the gathering of nations and their arrival at Armageddon — is a final, urgent warning. It uses the language of the **Bride's preparation**:

- "Keepeth his garments" = maintaining the righteousness of the Lord Jesus Christ
- "Walk naked" = without the covering of the Blood, exposed and shameful

Branham spoke often about the garments of righteousness and the call to watch:

> "Be sealed. Now, the Scripture, Ephesians 4:30, says this: 'Grieve not the Holy Spirit of God, whereby ye are sealed unto the day of redemption.'"
> — *1959-04-15, God's Provided Way, ¶66*

That this warning is placed here — at the very point where the world is gathering for its final destruction — underscores the urgency of the Message. If anyone reading Revelation during the tribulation can hear and heed this call, the mercy of Christ is still extended in this narrow moment.

## 5. Armageddon — The Battle of That Great Day of God Almighty

> "And he gathered them together into a place called in the Hebrew tongue Armageddon."
> — *Revelation 16:16 (KJV)*

**Armageddon** (the hill or plain of Megiddo, in northern Israel) is where the nations of the earth are gathered for the final battle. Branham taught this as the culmination of the antichrist campaign against Israel and against God:

> "Here comes Satan with all four corners of the earth with his Protestants, and with his Catholics, and all together, marching right up to the battle of Armageddon. All right. And here comes Jesus coming down from heaven with resurrected saints, vindicated Word."
> — *1963-03-21, Seals 6: The Fourth Seal, ¶129*

> "It'll finally wind up into the great battle of Armageddon. That'll be the last of it."
> — *1963-04-21, Victory Day, ¶50*

> "The battle started in heaven. It'll be finished on earth in the form of Armageddon."
> — *1963-03-21, Seals 6: The Fourth Seal, ¶110*

Armageddon is not a nuclear war between nation-states in the conventional sense — it is the final conflict between the Lamb and the dragon, between the Word of God and the spirit of antichrist, between the armies of heaven and the gathered forces of the deceived earth. Christ returns, and the battle is ended by His word of power (Rev 19:11-21).

## 6. Sixth Trumpet and Sixth Vial — Euphrates Twice

Both the **Sixth Trumpet** (Rev 9:13-16) and the **Sixth Vial** (Rev 16:12) involve the Euphrates River — but differently:

| Sixth Trumpet | Sixth Vial |
|---|---|
| Four angels **bound** at the Euphrates are **loosed** (Rev 9:14) | The Euphrates is **dried up** (Rev 16:12) |
| 200 million horsemen released to slay a third of men | Way prepared for kings of the east |
| Partial judgment — warning | Final gathering — execution |
| Targeted at Israel (trumpets = Israel) | Targeted at the whole world (vials = Gentile apostate world) |

Branham noted:

> "Under the sixth trumpet (Revelation 9:13), under the sixth trumpet, note, there was 200,000 horsemen, that had been bound in the river of Euphrates was turned loose under the sixth trumpet."
> — *1964-07-19, The Feast of the Trumpets, ¶66*

The Euphrates appears twice because it is the prophetic boundary between East and West, between the world of Israel's enemies and the Promised Land. The sixth vial removes that boundary completely — the whole world converges on the Middle East.
