# First Vial — Noisome and Grievous Sores
## Revelation 16:2

---

### Scripture (KJV)
> **Revelation 16:2** — And the first went, and poured out his vial upon the earth; and there fell a noisome and grievous sore upon the men which had the mark of the beast, and upon them which worshipped his image.

### Cross-Reference — The Sixth Plague of Egypt (KJV)
> **Exodus 9:9-11** — And it shall become small dust in all the land of Egypt, and shall be a boil breaking forth with blains upon man, and upon beast, throughout all the land of Egypt. And they took ashes of the furnace, and stood before Pharaoh; and Moses sprinkled it up toward heaven; and it became a boil breaking forth with blains upon man, and upon beast. And the magicians could not stand before Moses because of the boils; for the boil was upon the magicians, and upon all the Egyptians.

### Cross-Reference — Revelation 14:9-10 (KJV)
> And the third angel followed them, saying with a loud voice, If any man worship the beast and his image, and receive his mark in his forehead, or in his hand, The same shall drink of the wine of the wrath of God, which is poured out without mixture into the cup of his indignation; and he shall be tormented with fire and brimstone in the presence of the holy angels, and in the presence of the Lamb.

## 1. Target — Who Receives the First Vial

The first vial falls specifically upon **those who have the mark of the beast and worship his image**. This is a precise, judicial judgment — not a general calamity, but divine targeting of those who have actively aligned themselves with the beast system.

Branham's teaching on the mark of the beast is foundational to understanding this vial. The mark is not primarily a physical barcode or chip, but a **spiritual alignment** — the rejection of the Holy Spirit and of God's Word in favor of organized, denominational religion under the influence of Rome.

> "To receive the seal of God is to receive the Holy Ghost. To reject it is to have the mark of the beast. There's the whole thing."
> — *1959-06-28, Questions and Answers, ¶74*

> "The mark of the beast comes from Rome, throughout the world: Catholicism with Protestantism joined into it, organized religion. And they're going to unionize the churches, till every church will have to bow to that thing."
> — *1954-05-14, The Seal of God, ¶65*

> "It's already come. See? As soon as the Holy Ghost begin to fall, the mark of the beast begin to take place… You only have two things. One of them is, accept It, takes the Seal of God. To reject It, takes the mark of the beast."
> — *1963-06-30, Is Your Life Worthy of the Gospel?, ¶38*

> "When the Baptist church, when the Pentecostal church, when the Presbyterian church took on the mark of the beast instead of receiving the Holy Ghost, they went out from God and made them an organization."
> — *1961-02-16, The Seal of God and the Mark of the Beast Part 1, ¶58*

The first vial, therefore, falls upon the apostate religious world — all those who chose denominational structure, tradition, and the image of the beast over the living Word of God.

---

## 2. The Sore — Judgment Manifest

The plague is described as **noisome** (filthy, stinking, offensive) and **grievous** (severe, painful). This directly echoes the sixth plague of Egypt — the plague of boils that fell upon the Egyptians and their magicians while Israel was untouched in Goshen.

Branham taught that the plagues of Egypt are types and shadows of the coming judgments upon the apostate church. As Egypt typified the world system and bondage, and as Pharaoh typified the antichrist spirit, so the plagues of Egypt find their end-time fulfillment in the seven vials.

> "The same shall drink of the wine of the wrath of God, which is poured out without mixture into the cup of his indignation."
> — *Revelation 14:10 (KJV)*

Note that in Revelation 14:9-10, the angel warns that those who receive the mark **shall** drink the wine of God's wrath — the vials are the fulfillment of this pre-announced sentence. They are not arbitrary; they are judicial execution of a verdict already announced.

The Egyptian magicians were struck with the boil in Exodus 9 — those who had counterfeited the genuine miracles of God were singled out for this plague. In the same way, the first vial targets those who have **counterfeited the genuine move of the Holy Spirit** — the false church that has imitated the form of godliness while denying its power.

## 3. Parallel to the Sixth Plague of Egypt

| Egypt (Type) | Vial (Antitype) |
|---|---|
| Boils on man and beast throughout Egypt (Ex 9:9) | Grievous sore on those with mark of beast (Rev 16:2) |
| Israel in Goshen untouched | The Bride already raptured — untouched |
| Magicians could not stand (Ex 9:11) | False religious leaders powerless |
| Pharaoh's heart hardened | Beast's followers blaspheme rather than repent |

Branham taught the overall parallel structure:

> "All of it repeats at least once. For instance, in the Bible… it behooves us to kind of study about Babylon… Babylon appearing in the first of the Bible, in the middle of the Bible, and in the last of the Bible."
> — *1958-06-18, Handwriting on the Wall, ¶16*

## 4. Spiritual Interpretation

The sore manifests what is already spiritually true. Those who bear the mark of the beast are already spiritually diseased — rejecting the Word, living in spiritual fornication with the world, preaching creeds and traditions instead of the living Christ. The first vial makes that inward spiritual corruption manifest in physical judgment.

This follows the pattern of all divine judgment: God first speaks through His prophets, then through conviction of the Holy Spirit, then through physical signs, and finally through irrevocable judgment.

> "I never had Billy, my son, to say such to me as he did just a few moments ago. He said, 'Daddy… I never seen so many sick people.'"
> — *1965-04-26, Proving His Word, ¶5*

The world already staggers under sickness and disease as a witness. But the first vial, poured during the tribulation after the Bride is gone, will be something qualitatively different — direct judicial wrath without the mediating grace that is present today.

---

## 5. Timing

The first vial is poured during the **great tribulation**, after the rapture of the Bride. The Bride does not experience the vials:

> "The church will not go through the tribulation! Do you see? They was already in glory."
> — *1961-01-08, Revelation Chapter 4c: Throne of Mercy and Judgement, ¶85*

The mercy of God is exhausted toward the beast system before this vial falls. The temple in heaven is sealed with smoke — no man can enter — until all seven plagues are finished (Rev 15:8). Intercession has ceased for those who bear the mark.

---

## 6. A Warning, Not Simply a Prophecy

Branham always used the coming judgment as a present-tense warning. The vials are future in their literal pouring out, but the sealing that determines who receives them is happening **now**:

> "The mark of the beast is on today, and the seal of God is the Holy Spirit. To reject it is the mark of the beast."
> — *1959-06-28, Questions and Answers, ¶74*

Receive the Seal of God — the Holy Spirit — and you will not be present when the first vial falls. Reject it, align with the religious beast system, and the noisome and grievous sore awaits.
