## Quick Reference — Key Message Teachings

### The Vials Are Not for the Bride
The Bride of Christ is raptured before the vials are poured. The vials fall during the great tribulation upon the apostate world.

> "The church will not go through the tribulation! Do you see? They was already in glory."
> — *1961-01-08, Revelation Chapter 4c: Throne of Mercy and Judgement, ¶85*

### The Vials Run Parallel to the Seals, Trumpets, and Church Ages
> "They all happened right at the same time, following the plagues, following the church ages just the same, because it is all sealed up into that one book. Everything happens just in rotation."
> — *1963-03-19, Seals 4: The Second Seal, ¶118*

### The Seventh Seal Contains the Vials
> "Right in that seventh seal there pours out vials, and everything else are poured out."
> — *1963-03-21, Seals 6: The Fourth Seal, ¶47*

### Mystery Babylon = Roman Catholic Church
> "She was called 'Mystery, Babylon' (we know that's the Catholic church), but notice, she's the mother of harlots."
> — *1960-09-11, Five Definite Identifications of the True Church, ¶36*

### The Seat of the Beast = Rome
> "That's where it began right there, in Rome, and that's where the seat of the beast is, and from there come the old mother whore."
> — *1964-08-23, Questions and Answers Part 1, ¶162*

### The Three Frog-Spirits = Trinitarian Doctrine from Nicaea
> "Three unclean spirits like frogs… a trinity doctrine against the truth… They look back to the Nicaea Council where the trinity doctrine was born at — not in the Bible."
> — *1965-07-25, The Anointed Ones at the End Time, ¶172, 174*
