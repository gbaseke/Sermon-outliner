# COUNTDOWN TO JUDGEMENT - Preacher Notes
**Date:** 2026-03-04 | **Core Scriptures:** John 5:22-24; Romans 8:1; 2 Corinthians 5:10

---

## OPENING QUESTION
What is judgement, who is the Judge, and how does receiving Jesus Christ change everything about where you stand on that great and terrible Day?

---

## THIS HOUR
Jesus has stepped from mercy seat to judgement seat. White wig = Supreme Judge. Bride already judged at Calvary—stands FREE. World rushes to great white throne. This is comfort AND warning.

---

## INTRODUCTION
**Human scene:** Everyone knows judgement is coming—conscience, fear, restlessness.  
**Type:** Prophet/Priest/King—Jesus fulfills all three offices. Now: JUDGE.  
**Person:** Jesus Christ—qualified Judge who walked in our shoes.

---

## POINT 1 - ALL JUDGEMENT COMMITTED TO THE SON
**Key:** John 5:22 - Father committed all judgment to the Son

- Your Judge is not a stranger—He's the Bridegroom who bled for you
- Same hand that holds gavel was pierced for your sins
- God is no respecter of persons (Acts 10:34)
- **Quote:** "He is the judge, the supreme judge" (1960-12-04)

---

## POINT 2 - BELIEVER ALREADY JUDGED
**Key:** John 5:24 - "shall not come into condemnation; passed from death unto life"  
**Key:** Romans 8:1 - "no condemnation to them in Christ Jesus"

- **Type:** Passover blood (Exodus 12)—death angel passed over those under blood
- Your judgement fell on the Lamb at Calvary
- God cannot justly judge you again—already settled
- NOT by church membership—by baptism of Holy Ghost into His Body (1 Cor 12:13)
- **Quote:** "God can't justly judge it again, for it's already judged" (1957-04-19)

---

## POINT 3 - CATEGORIES OF JUDGEMENT
**Key:** 2 Corinthians 5:10; Revelation 20:6

**Three groups, three courts:**
- **Bride:** First resurrection, no second death, WITH the Judge (not before Him)
- **Foolish virgins:** Saved in tribulation, sheep on right hand (Matt 25:33-34)
- **Wicked:** Rejected the Word, goats on left, great white throne

**Quote:** "She's not before the judgment seat—she is at the Judge's side" (1961-10-15)

---

## POINT 4 - FOUR SOULS AT THE CROSSROADS
**Key:** John 12:48 - "the word that I have spoken, same shall judge him"

**a) Judas:** Light + opportunity + calling = REJECTED for 30 silver  
**b) Rich young ruler:** Sincere but no surrender. Close ≠ in the Kingdom  
**c) Thief on cross:** Nothing but faith—and faith was enough  
**d) Rich man/Lazarus:** Religion without reality. Called Abraham "Father" but never knew God

**Principle:** Judgement measured by: Light received, opportunity given, what you did with it

---

## POINT 5 - STANDARD IS THE WORD
**Key:** John 12:48; John 1:1

- Not Catholic, Baptist, Methodist—THE WORD judges
- Jesus and Word are ONE
- "The day you eat thereof, that day you die"—God's filter is His Word
- **Quote:** "Not a denomination, not a creed...but a thinking man's filter! For you're going to be judged by the Word" (1965-08-22)

---

## POINT 6 - SAINTS SHALL JUDGE THE WORLD
**Key:** 1 Corinthians 6:2 - "Know ye not that the saints shall judge the earth?"

- **Type:** Daniel 7:18—saints possess the kingdom forever
- Bride not acquitted only—SEATED with Judge
- Like Esther with the King, Joseph on throne
- Your faithfulness = standard by which nations measured
- **Quote:** "Here's the bride, the attorneys" (1960-12-11)

---

## CONCLUSION - THE EPIPHANY
**Judgement is:** The Word of God applied in righteousness by Jesus Christ  
**For the believer:** No condemnation, no countdown to fear. Judgement fell on Calvary.  
**When He sits as Judge:** You stand BESIDE Him, not before Him

**Six movements:**
1. Jesus is the only qualified Judge
2. Believer already judged at Calvary
3. Three categories: Bride, foolish virgins, wicked
4. Four souls show judgement by light/opportunity/response
5. Standard is the WORD, not church
6. Saints judge the world—reign with Christ

**Heart:** Countdown to judgement = reason for DECISION. Receive Christ, be hid in His body. Judgement that shakes world CROWNS the Bride.

**Closing Hymn:** "My Hope Is Built On Nothing Less"

---

**ALTAR CALL:** Where do you stand? Under the blood or under judgement? This is your hour. Come now.
