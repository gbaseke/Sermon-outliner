## THIS HOUR --- PROPHETIC ANCHOR

We are living in the transition hour. The Lamb is leaving the mercy seat. Intercession is closing and the Lion of the tribe of Judah is about to take the title deed of redemption and claim His Bride. Every overcomer in this end-time Message is being prepared not merely to escape the tribulation, but to sit with Him in judgment and to rule the nations with a rod of iron. The revelation of the rod is the revelation of the Word --- and the Bride who has the Word has the authority. This sermon places the believer squarely between the promise and the fulfillment: the rod has been given, the throne is being set, and the Bride must know what she holds in her hand.

---

## INTRODUCTION --- SETTING THE TYPE

**Tone:** Warm and pastoral --- open the heart before opening the Word.

### The Human Scene (Humanistic)

The world is looking for power. Every nation, every political system, every religious organization is grasping for control. Rome wants it. The councils of churches want it. The governments want it. And every human heart, if we are honest, wants to feel that it has some authority, some standing, some say in the way things turn out. We live in an hour where men feel powerless --- powerless against the government, powerless against sickness, powerless against the moral collapse of the world. And right in the middle of that helplessness, the Lord Jesus speaks to His Church and says: "To him that overcometh will I give power over the nations." Not political power. Not military power. But something far greater --- the rod of iron, which is the very Word of God.

### The Type Established

- **Shadow (OT):** Moses' rod --- a dry stick in the hand of a surrendered man, which became God's judgment rod over Egypt and the instrument that smote the rock to bring forth water.
- **Substance (NT/End-time):** The Word of God proceeding from the mouth of Christ --- the sharp two-edged sword by which He smites the nations and rules them with a rod of iron. And by extension, the Bride who carries that same Word carries that same authority.
- **Branham on this type:**
  > "And the rod that Moses had in his hand, it was not the rod of Moses, it was the rod of God: God's judgment rod. Moses held it out over Egypt and flies came up. He held it out and fleas came up. Wherever he dumped this rod, it was a judgment rod. It brought judgment upon a disobedient people. And the rod was Christ, the Substitute for sin and sickness."
  > *--- 1950-08-27 -- Take the rod and gather the people -- p.21*

### Person Profile: The Overcomer (Revelation 2:26-27)

- **Visible need:** Strength to endure --- the Thyatira age was marked by the depth of Satan's seat, Jezebel's teaching, and the darkest period of church history.
- **Invisible need:** Not merely survival, but authority --- the overcomer needed to know that the suffering had a purpose and a throne at the end of it.
- **Motive:** Faithfulness to the Word in the face of death.
- **Intention:** To keep His works unto the end --- not their own works, but His.
- **What God saw:** A joint-heir. One who would sit with Christ and break the nations. God saw a king and a priest inside that persecuted believer.

## POINT 1 --- THE ROD IDENTIFIED: THE WORD OF GOD

**Key Scripture:** "And out of his mouth goeth a sharp sword, that with it he should smite the nations: and he shall rule them with a rod of iron." --- Revelation 19:15

### The Human Need (Humanistic)

- People want to know: What is the rod of iron? Is it a literal weapon? Is it force?
- The Jews in Jesus' day were confused about this very thing. They expected a military Messiah with a literal rod, stomping out Rome.
- The world still thinks power comes from weapons, politics, and force. But the rod of iron is not made of metal --- it comes out of His mouth.
- Every believer who has ever felt weak against the world's system needs to understand what weapon they actually carry.

### Divine Revelation

**The Word says:**
> "For the word of God is quick, and powerful, and sharper than any twoedged sword, piercing even to the dividing asunder of soul and spirit, and of the joints and marrow, and is a discerner of the thoughts and intents of the heart." --- Hebrews 4:12

**The type unfolds:**

When Moses stood before Pharaoh, he had nothing but a dry stick. But that stick was the rod of God. It was God's judgment rod. When Moses stretched it over the Red Sea, the sea parted. When he struck the rock, water came forth for a dying people. That rod was not Moses' ability --- it was God's authority placed in a surrendered hand. Now, in Revelation 19, we see the antitype: Christ rides forth and out of His mouth goes not a sword of steel but the sharp sword of the Word. The rod of iron is the Word of God. It is the same Word that created the heavens and the earth, the same Word that said "Let there be light," the same Word that was made flesh and dwelt among us. And the nations that refuse it will be dashed in pieces like a potter's vessel.

**From the Message:**
> "A sharp two-edged sword. What was going from the mouth of this person? The Word of God. It's a sharp two-edged sword. What was it doing? Discerning the thoughts of the heart, the intents ... goes even deeper than the meat, the flesh, the blood cells, into the bone, down into the marrow of the bone, plumb on beyond that --- even a discerner of the thoughts and intents of the heart."
> *--- 1960-12-04 -- Church Ages, part 2: The Patmos Vision -- p.119*

**Sub-points:**
- The rod of iron is the Word --- it proceeds from His mouth (Rev. 19:15)
- Moses' rod was a type of this --- God's judgment rod in a surrendered vessel (Exodus 4:2-4, 17:5-6)
- The first rider on the white horse (Rev. 6:2) had a bow but no arrows --- a bluffer. Christ's white horse has both lightning and thunder: the Word.
- The rod does not merely threaten --- it executes. "The Word is going to kill them. You know that." (Branham, Seals 6)

### THUS SAITH THE LORD --- The Vision

The Bride of Christ in this hour carries the same rod that Moses carried, the same sword that proceeds from the mouth of the Lord Jesus. It is the vindicated Word for this day. Every revelation you receive from this Message, every Thus Saith the Lord that takes root in your heart, is a stroke of that iron rod against the kingdoms of this world. Do not despise the simplicity of the Word. The nations will not be broken by armies. They will be broken by the Word that you carry. The rod is already in your hand. Do you know what you are holding?

## POINT 2 --- THE LAMB BECOMES THE LION: FROM INTERCESSOR TO KING

**Key Scripture:** "And I saw in the right hand of him that sat on the throne a book written within and on the backside, sealed with seven seals. And I saw a strong angel proclaiming with a loud voice, Who is worthy to open the book, and to loose the seals thereof?" --- Revelation 5:1-2

### The Human Need (Humanistic)

- We have known Jesus as Saviour, as Healer, as Baptizer. We have come to Him as the Lamb who was slain. But many believers have never seen Him as the Lion.
- There is a transition taking place. The mercy seat is becoming the judgment seat.
- People wonder: Why has judgment not come yet? Why does evil still prosper? Because the Lamb has been interceding. But when He steps forth from that throne, He is no longer a Lamb --- He is a Lion, and He comes to rule.

### Divine Revelation

**The Word says:**
> "And one of the elders saith unto me, Weep not: behold, the Lion of the tribe of Juda, the Root of David, hath prevailed to open the book, and to loose the seven seals thereof." --- Revelation 5:5

**The type unfolds:**

In the Old Testament, when the kinsman redeemer had finished paying the price, he stepped forward to claim the inheritance. Boaz paid the price at the gate of Bethlehem and took Ruth as his bride. But before Ruth could sit with Boaz, the transaction had to be completed publicly. So it is with Christ. He paid the price at Calvary. He has been interceding as the slain Lamb for nearly two thousand years. But now He rises from the throne of the Father, takes the Book, breaks the Seals, and the Lamb becomes the Lion. He has the title deed of redemption. He calls for His Bride to stand by His side. And together --- King and Queen --- they rule the nations with a rod of iron.

**From the Message:**
> "Notice, here He comes --- leaves the throne as an intercessor, as a slain lamb, to become a lion, King, to bring the whole world into judgment, that's rejected. Our kinsman redeemer then is King over all. Why? He's got the title deed of redemption. It all lays within his hand."
> *--- 1963-03-17 -- Seals 2: Breach between the Church Ages and the Seals -- p.136*

> "He leaves the throne to be an intercessor as a slain Lamb to be a Lion, King, to bring the world to judgment who has rejected his message. He's not a mediator ... When the Lamb slain walked forward from eternity out of the Father's throne and took his right, it was a judgment seat! Then He become not a lamb, but a lion. King! And He calls for his queen to come stand by his side."
> *--- 1963-03-17 -- Seals 2: Breach between the Church Ages and the Seals -- p.132*

**Sub-points:**
- The Lamb and the Lion are the same Person --- but two offices, two dispensations
- When the blood leaves the mercy seat, it becomes the judgment seat (there is no more intercession)
- Boaz is the type: kinsman redeemer who finishes the transaction and takes his bride --- then rules
- "Know ye not the saints shall judge the earth?" (I Corinthians 6:2) --- this is not future theory; it is the Bride's destiny

### THUS SAITH THE LORD --- The Vision

Church, the transition is happening now. We are not waiting for some distant event. The Seals have been opened. The mysteries have been revealed. The Lamb is stepping forth. And when He stands as King, He will not stand alone --- His Queen will be at His side. Every trial you overcome, every temptation you resist by the Word, every time you stand faithful when the world and the church world both reject the Message --- you are being qualified to rule. The rod of iron is not given to spectators. It is given to overcomers.

## POINT 3 --- THE OVERCOMER'S REWARD: POWER OVER THE NATIONS

**Key Scripture:** "And he that overcometh, and keepeth my works unto the end, to him will I give power over the nations: And he shall rule them with a rod of iron; as the vessels of a potter shall they be broken to shivers: even as I received of my Father." --- Revelation 2:26-27

### The Human Need (Humanistic)

- What does it mean to overcome? Many think it means quitting cigarettes or cleaning up your language. And yes, there is a moral transformation. But overcoming is far deeper than behavior modification.
- The overcomer is not the one who merely cleaned up --- it is the one who held the Word when everything and everyone said to let it go.
- We live in a Laodicean hour where Christ Himself has been put outside the church. The overcomer in this age is the one who opens the door and lets Him back in.

### Divine Revelation

**The Word says:**
> "To him that overcometh will I grant to sit with me in my throne, even as I also overcame, and am set down with my Father in his throne." --- Revelation 3:21

**The type unfolds:**

In each church age, the overcomer received a specific reward. To Ephesus: the tree of life. To Smyrna: freedom from the second death. To Pergamos: hidden manna and a white stone. But to Thyatira --- right in the darkest hour, when Satan's seat was fully established, when the Roman system was killing the saints --- the reward was power over the nations. A rod of iron. The very thing Satan used against them (political and religious power) would be given back into their hands through Christ. The potter's vessel --- the man-made, fragile system of this world --- will be dashed to pieces by the overcoming Bride.

**From the Message:**
> "Christ is to rule the nations with a rod of iron, and here is the church that overcome setting in there with Him to break the nations with a rod of iron."
> *--- 1963-08-25 -- How can I overcome? -- p.92*

> "If you notice, it said, 'She has made herself ready.' ... 'They that overcome shall inherit all things.' --- they that overcome. You have power to do it, but you must be willing to lay it down. She has made herself ready."
> *--- 1962-01-21 -- The Marriage of the Lamb -- p.28*

**Sub-points:**
- Overcoming is not passive --- "She has made herself ready" (Rev. 19:7)
- The reward of the overcomer is proportional to the darkness of the age --- the darker the hour, the greater the authority given
- "Even as I received of my Father" --- the authority is delegated. Christ received it from the Father; the Bride receives it from Christ
- The potter's vessel represents man-made systems (religious, political, social) that will be shattered by the Word

### THUS SAITH THE LORD --- The Vision

Saint of God, do not grow weary. The very thing that is pressing you --- the rejection, the loneliness, the scorn of the denominational world, the mockery of family and friends who do not understand this Message --- that pressure is producing an overcomer. And the overcomer does not merely survive. The overcomer sits on the throne. The Bride of this hour will not be a spectator in the millennium. She will rule with Him. The nations that rejected the Word will be subject to the Word. What the world took from you, Christ will give back a thousandfold --- not in kind, but in authority.

## POINT 4 --- THE MILLENNIUM REIGN: CHRIST AND HIS BRIDE ON THE THRONE

**Key Scripture:** "And I saw thrones, and they sat upon them, and judgment was given unto them: and I saw the souls of them that were beheaded for the witness of Jesus, and for the word of God, and which had not worshipped the beast, neither his image, neither had received his mark upon their foreheads, or in their hands; and they lived and reigned with Christ a thousand years." --- Revelation 20:4

### The Human Need (Humanistic)

- People want to know: What will the millennium actually look like? Will there be nations? Will there be people? Who will the Bride rule over?
- There is confusion in the religious world about the millennium. Some deny it altogether. Some spiritualize it away. But the Bible is plain: there is a literal thousand-year reign of Christ and His Bride upon this earth.
- Believers need a vision of their future. Not pie-in-the-sky fantasy, but Word-anchored hope.

### Divine Revelation

**The Word says:**
> "Blessed and holy is he that hath part in the first resurrection: on such the second death hath no power, but they shall be priests of God and of Christ, and shall reign with him a thousand years." --- Revelation 20:6

**The type unfolds:**

Joseph is a perfect type. Rejected by his brothers, sold into slavery, cast into prison --- but raised up to sit at the right hand of Pharaoh and rule all of Egypt. And when the famine came, every nation had to come to Joseph. They bowed their knee to the one they had rejected. So Christ, rejected by Israel and by the Gentile church world, will reign from the throne of David, and the nations will bow. And the Bride --- who suffered with Him in rejection --- will sit with Him in authority. The heathens who never heard the gospel will be raised in that time, and the manifested sons of God will have authority over them, even as Brother Branham taught.

**From the Message:**
> "And the heathens that's never heard the gospel shall be raised in that time, and the sons of God will be manifested. If he's to reign, he's got to have something to reign over, he's got a domain. 'And they ruled and reigned with Christ,' and Christ ruled with a rod of iron, the nations."
> *--- 1962-05-27 -- Questions and Answers -- p.72*

> "He came first to redeem His bride, comes next to receive His bride, comes next with His bride, as King and queen, to reign through the Millennium."
> *--- 1960-08-03 -- Abraham -- p.39*

**Sub-points:**
- The millennium is literal --- a thousand years of Christ's reign upon the earth
- The Bride is raised in the first resurrection and reigns with Him (Rev. 20:4-6)
- The nations ruled are those who survived the tribulation and the heathens raised who never heard the gospel
- The manifested sons of God will carry authority just like Jesus had when He was here on earth
- The earth is purified by fire before the millennium begins (Armageddon cleanses the earth)

### THUS SAITH THE LORD --- The Vision

The Bride is being prepared for a throne. Everything you walk through now is training for reigning. The Word you receive, the battles you fight, the character that is formed in you by the Holy Ghost --- it is all qualification. You are not just getting ready to leave this world. You are getting ready to come back and rule it. King and Queen, Christ and His Bride, with the rod of iron --- the Word of God --- ruling the nations in righteousness for a thousand years. That is your destiny. Walk worthy of it.

## POINT 5 --- THE TWO GOG AND MAGOG: ARMAGEDDON AND THE FINAL BATTLE

**Key Scripture:** "And when the thousand years are expired, Satan shall be loosed out of his prison, And shall go out to deceive the nations which are in the four quarters of the earth, Gog and Magog, to gather them together to battle: the number of whom is as the sand of the sea." --- Revelation 20:7-8

### The Human Need (Humanistic)

- Many sincere believers confuse the battle of Armageddon with the battle of Gog and Magog after the millennium. They read Ezekiel 38-39 and Revelation 20 and think they are the same event.
- The preacher's note is clear: there are two Gog and Magog events. This must be taught plainly so the congregation is not confused by denominational teaching or newspaper prophecy.
- Understanding the prophetic timeline gives the believer confidence and removes fear.

### Divine Revelation

**The Word says:**
> "And he gathered them together into a place called in the Hebrew tongue Armageddon." --- Revelation 16:16

> "And they went up on the breadth of the earth, and compassed the camp of the saints about, and the beloved city: and fire came down from God out of heaven, and devoured them." --- Revelation 20:9

**The type unfolds:**

The millennium itself is typed by Noah's passage through the flood. Noah was carried over in the ark, and on the other side of the flood there was a new world --- but sin came through in Ham. Enoch, who was translated before the flood, is a type of the Bride who goes up in the rapture. Noah is a type of the remnant carried through the tribulation into the millennium. But even after the flood, sin was present. So after the millennium, when Satan is loosed, he finds subjects willing to follow him --- those who have lived under the rule of the rod but whose hearts were never changed. This is the final test, and fire from heaven settles it once and for all.

**From the Message:**
> "That is not the battle of Armageddon. The battle of Armageddon takes place on this side, when the tribulation period is ended. Now, what relation does this have with the battle of Gog and Magog? None. One is this thousand years, and the other one is the end of the thousand years ... Two battles altogether."
> *--- 1963-03-24 -- Seals 9: Questions and Answers on the Seals -- p.166*

> "The millennium is a type of Noah going in the ark, and carried over, and brought Ham and them on the other side. And sin even come out of the ark. But Enoch, the translated one, was a type of the bride that went up, not the one that was carried over. So sin will still be on the other side of the millennium, but not during the millennium."
> *--- 1964-08-30 -- Questions and Answers, Part 3 -- p.80*

**Sub-points:**
- **Gog and Magog #1 (pre-millennium):** Russia and allies come against Israel --- this is linked to Ezekiel 38-39 and occurs in connection with Armageddon, before the millennium, after the rapture of the Bride
- **Gog and Magog #2 (post-millennium):** Satan is loosed after the thousand years, gathers the wicked from the four corners of the earth, and compasses the camp of the saints --- fire from God destroys them (Rev. 20:7-9)
- These are two completely separate events, separated by a thousand years
- The rod of iron keeps order during the millennium --- but the final rebellion proves that even a perfect environment cannot change a heart without the new birth
- After the second Gog and Magog: the Great White Throne judgment, the lake of fire, then the new heavens and new earth

### THUS SAITH THE LORD --- The Vision

Here is the sobering truth, Church. A thousand years of perfect government under the rod of iron --- and still, when Satan is loosed, he finds an army willing to follow him. That tells you something. Environment does not save. Education does not save. Good government does not save. Only the new birth saves. Only the Word of God planted in the soul by the Holy Ghost produces an overcomer. That is why this Message is so vital. That is why the rod of iron is not a rod of legislation or religion --- it is the rod of the living Word. And those who have it within them will never be deceived, not in a thousand years, not in ten thousand, not in eternity. Because it is not external rule --- it is internal life.

## CONCLUSION

### The Epiphany --- The Question Answered

> What is the rod of iron, who will wield it, and what does it mean for us --- the Bride of this hour --- that we have been promised power over the nations?
>
> "The rod of iron is the Word of God --- the same Word that proceeds from the mouth of Christ, the same Word that Moses carried as a dry stick that became the judgment rod of God. It will be wielded by Christ and His Bride together, King and Queen, in the millennium reign. And for us in this hour, it means that the Word we carry is not a dead letter --- it is the very authority of Almighty God. Every overcomer who keeps His works unto the end is being handed a rod of iron. Not for destruction, but for righteous rule. Not for revenge, but for the restoration of all things under the headship of Jesus Christ."

### Summary

- **Point 1:** The rod of iron is the Word of God --- it proceeds from His mouth, not from human force.
- **Point 2:** Christ is transitioning from Lamb to Lion, from Intercessor to King, and He calls His Queen to stand beside Him.
- **Point 3:** The overcomer's reward is power over the nations --- the darker the age, the greater the authority given to those who stand.
- **Point 4:** The millennium is a literal thousand-year reign where the Bride, as joint-heir, rules with Christ over the nations.
- **Point 5:** Two Gog and Magog events --- one before and one after the millennium --- prove that only the new birth, not environment, can produce a true overcomer.

> **The heart of this message:** The rod of iron is the Word, and the Bride who carries the Word carries the throne.

### A Closing Hymn

**Suggested hymn:** "A Charge to Keep I Have"
**Why:** This hymn speaks of faithfulness, of keeping the charge that God has given, of running the race and overcoming --- the very spirit of the overcomer who is promised power over the nations.
