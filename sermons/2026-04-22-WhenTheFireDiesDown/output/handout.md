# When the Fire Dies Down
### A Pastoral Message on Burnout

**Date:** 2026-04-22
**Core Text:** *"It is enough."* — 1 Kings 19:4

---

## Take-Home Truth

> **God's greatest servants hit the wall.**
> **Not one of them was shamed for it.**
> **He fed them, let them sleep, and whispered.**

---

## The Four Voices of the Weary

| Servant | Scripture | Cry |
|---------|-----------|-----|
| Moses | Num 11:14 | *"It is too heavy for me"* |
| Elijah | 1 Kgs 19:4 | *"Take away my life"* |
| Jeremiah | Jer 20:9 | *"I will not speak any more"* |
| Paul | 2 Cor 1:8 | *"We despaired even of life"* |

---

## Elijah's Journey — Our Pattern

1. **Mount Carmel** — the great victory
2. **The Juniper Tree** — the crash that follows
3. **Food and Sleep** — God's first remedy
4. **The Cave** — honest prayer
5. **The Still Small Voice** — gentle restoration

---

## Five Promises to Hold Tonight

- **Isaiah 40:31** — *"They that wait upon the LORD shall renew their strength."*
- **Matthew 11:28** — *"Come unto me... I will give you rest."*
- **1 Peter 5:7** — *"Casting all your care upon him; for he careth for you."*
- **Psalm 55:22** — *"He shall sustain thee."*
- **Galatians 6:9** — *"In due season we shall reap, if we faint not."*
