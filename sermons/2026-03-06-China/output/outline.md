# YOU CANNOT STOP THE SUN FROM MOVING

**Scripture(s):** Daniel 12:4; Proverbs 24:5; Ecclesiastes 1:18 | **Theme:** The futility of opposing God's sovereign purpose | **Prepared:** 2026-03-06

---

## THE OPENING QUESTION

> Can you stop the purpose of God? When a man, a nation, or an empire shifts
> from pursuing excellence to hindering others from rising --- what does God
> say about that, and what does He do with it?

---

## THIS HOUR --- PROPHETIC ANCHOR

We are living in the hour when civilization has completed its westward journey with the sun and is pressing back toward the east. The nations are scrambling --- not to build, but to restrain. Trade wars, chip bans, tariffs --- the spirit of Pharaoh is alive: "They are multiplying; we must stop them." But nothing can defeat the purpose of God. He sealed this book until the end-time, and now knowledge is increasing exactly as Daniel prophesied. The Bride of Christ is not called to compete with the world or to fear its upheavals, but to recognize the sovereign hand moving all things toward the coming of the Lord Jesus Christ.

---

## INTRODUCTION --- SETTING THE TYPE

**Tone:** Warm and pastoral --- open the heart before opening the Word.

### The Human Scene (Humanistic)

Picture this: a great empire, the most powerful the world has ever known, looks across the water and sees a civilization --- older, patient, rising. And instead of saying, "Let me run my own race better," the empire says, "I must stop them." Chip bans. Rare earth restrictions. Tariff walls. Sanctions. It is no longer about being good --- it is about preventing someone else from being good. And you know, beloved, that spirit did not start in Washington or Beijing. It started in the Garden of Eden when Cain looked at Abel's accepted sacrifice and, instead of correcting his own offering, he slew his brother.

### The Type Established
- **Shadow (OT):** Pharaoh seeing Israel multiply and commanding the death of the male children (Exodus 1:9-16)
- **Substance (NT/End-time):** Every system that rises against God's purpose --- whether political, religious, or spiritual --- is doomed to fail. The more they afflict the seed, the more it multiplies.
- **Branham on this type:**
  > "There arose up a pharaoh who did not know Joseph, and he was a smart pharaoh, perhaps trained in all the theology of Egypt: smart, shrewd. But he didn't have... He had a good reasoning, but he didn't have the Word of God. Now, he reasoned it out scientifically and said, 'Looky here. They're all multiplying. They're more of a thrifty people than we are; they can live on a smaller income, and they're gathering and multiplying. So, the thing we must do is stop this right away, kill all the infant babies, males, so that there'll be no more...'"
  > *--- 1955-06-04 - Jairus, the secret believer - p11*

### Person Profile: Pharaoh (The One Who Fears the Rising of Another)
- **Visible need:** National security --- Egypt's stability seemed threatened by Israel's growth
- **Invisible need:** A heart submitted to God's sovereign plan --- Pharaoh could not see that Israel's blessing was God's doing
- **Motive:** Self-preservation disguised as national interest
- **Intention:** To suppress Israel's growth and maintain Egyptian supremacy
- **What God saw:** A vessel of wrath, fitted for destruction, through whom God would display His mighty power (Romans 9:17)

## POINT 1 --- CIVILIZATION TRAVELS WITH THE SUN

**Key Scripture:** "But thou, O Daniel, shut up the words, and seal the book, even to the time of the end: many shall run to and fro, and knowledge shall be increased." --- Daniel 12:4

### The Human Need (Humanistic)
- We live in a world obsessed with who is ahead and who is behind. Nations measure their worth by GDP, by military strength, by technological output.
- China is the oldest civilization known to man. For thousands of years, knowledge and culture flowed westward --- through Mesopotamia, through Greece and Rome, through Europe, across the Atlantic to America. Now it is pressing back toward the east.
- And the great question on every newscast, in every policy paper, in every trade negotiation is: "Can we stop this? Can we hold onto our position?"
- But beloved, has anyone asked God what He thinks about it?

### Divine Revelation
**The Word says:**
> "The LORD hath made all things for himself: yea, even the wicked for the day of evil." --- Proverbs 16:4

**The type unfolds:**
The movement of civilization with the sun is not an accident of history --- it is a pattern ordained by God. Just as the s-u-n rises in the east and sets in the west, so the S-o-n of God shone first upon Eastern people. The gospel traveled westward. And now we stand at the place where east and west have met. Brother Branham saw this pattern clearly and traced it from China all the way to the West Coast. No president, no policy, no tariff can redirect the sun. It moves by divine appointment.

**From the Message:**
> "Now, remember, civilization has traveled with the sun. The oldest civilization is the eastern civilization. China's the oldest civilization we know of. All right. Civilization's traveled with it. Now it's gone so far, till the east and west coast has met. We're at the west coast. If we went any further, we'd be back east again. When you leave California, you go on into Japan and China. You come back again. What is it? It shall be light in the evening time."
> *--- 1961-01-19 - Queen of Sheba - p58*

**Sub-points:**
- A. God set knowledge in motion, and He alone determines where it flows
- B. The oldest civilizations are in the East --- this was God's starting point
- C. America's position at the western edge of this journey is not permanent --- it is prophetic. It marks the end of the Gentile dispensation.

### THUS SAITH THE LORD --- The Vision
The same God who ordained that civilization would travel with the sun from east to west has ordained the evening light. Bride of Christ, do not be troubled by the headlines. The shifting of power between nations is just the ticking of God's great timepiece. He is not wringing His hands in heaven. He is bringing all things to their appointed end --- and that end is the coming of the Lord Jesus for His Bride.

## POINT 2 --- THE SIN OF STOPPING OTHERS INSTEAD OF RUNNING YOUR RACE

**Key Scripture:** "For in much wisdom is much grief: and he that increaseth knowledge increaseth sorrow." --- Ecclesiastes 1:18

### The Human Need (Humanistic)
- There is a moment in the life of every person, every church, every nation when a subtle and deadly shift takes place: the goal stops being "How do I become better?" and becomes "How do I stop him from becoming better?"
- America once led the world in innovation --- not by suppressing others, but by building. Now, chip bans, export controls, tariff walls --- the strategy has become containment. "If I cannot stay ahead, at least I will hold you back."
- And you know, saints, this same spirit creeps into families, into churches, into marriages. When you stop cultivating your own garden and start poisoning your neighbor's field, something has gone terribly wrong in your heart.
- It is the spirit of insecurity. And insecurity is nothing but unbelief dressed in a suit.

### Divine Revelation
**The Word says:**
> "Let us not be desirous of vain glory, provoking one another, envying one another." --- Galatians 5:26

> "For where envying and strife is, there is confusion and every evil work." --- James 3:16

**The type unfolds:**
This is the spirit of Cain. God accepted Abel's sacrifice and rejected Cain's. Now, the right response would have been for Cain to go back and correct his offering --- to get right with God. But instead, Cain turned his energy toward destroying the one God had blessed. The first murder in human history was not born of hatred alone --- it was born of jealousy. And jealousy is the fruit of insecurity. Cain was insecure because he knew, deep down, that his offering was wrong. Rather than fix it, he killed the man whose offering was right.

**From the Message:**
> "And we find that the first reason was because of jealousy, that Cain was jealous of Abel because that God accepted Abel's sacrifice. And Hebrews 11 tells us, that Abel offered unto God a more excellent sacrifice than that of Cain, and God testifying by it; and Cain, 'course, being jealous of this, because his sacrifice was rejected and Abel's accepted."
> *--- 1963-04-21 - Victory Day - p17*

**Sub-points:**
- A. Insecurity makes you a reactor instead of a creator --- you spend your energy responding to others rather than building what God has placed in your hand
- B. Jealousy is the confession that you have left the will of God --- a man walking in his calling has no need to tear down another man's calling
- C. Worldly knowledge without the Spirit produces this exact sorrow: you know enough to see who is rising, but you have no faith to trust God's sovereign arrangement

### THUS SAITH THE LORD --- The Vision
Church, examine your own heart tonight. Are you running your race, or are you watching somebody else's lane? The Bride of Christ has no competitors. She is not in a contest with the denominations, not in a rivalry with the world. She has one assignment: to be filled with the Word, to be clothed in the righteousness of Christ, and to be ready when He comes. If you are spending your energy criticizing another ministry, another brother, another sister --- you have taken your eyes off the prize. Get back in your lane. Fix your own offering. Let God be God.

## POINT 3 --- THE FEAR THAT BREEDS DESTRUCTION

**Key Scripture:** "The fear of man bringeth a snare: but whoso putteth his trust in the LORD shall be safe." --- Proverbs 29:25

### The Human Need (Humanistic)
- Pharaoh saw the Hebrews multiplying and his heart was gripped with fear: "They are more and mightier than we." So he enslaved them. Then he killed their babies. And what happened? God raised up Moses from the very house of Pharaoh.
- Herod heard that a King was born in Bethlehem, and instead of worshipping, he slaughtered every child under two years of age. And what happened? The Christ escaped to Egypt, grew up, and accomplished everything the Father sent Him to do.
- The bamboo curtain. The iron curtain. The chip ban. The tariff wall. Every barrier erected by fear has one thing in common: it has never, not once in all of human history, stopped the purpose of God.

### Divine Revelation
**The Word says:**
> "There is no wisdom nor understanding nor counsel against the LORD." --- Proverbs 21:30

> "And we know that all things work together for good to them that love God, to them who are the called according to his purpose." --- Romans 8:28

**The type unfolds:**
Pharaoh's fear of Israel produced the very deliverer of Israel. He commanded the male babies to be drowned in the Nile, and it was the Nile that carried baby Moses in a basket right to the door of Pharaoh's own daughter. The devil is a strategist, but God is sovereign. When Herod murdered the innocents, it was the fulfillment of Jeremiah's prophecy. The enemy's worst attacks become instruments in God's hands. The more they afflicted Israel, the more Israel multiplied. The more they tried to stop the Seed, the faster the Seed advanced.

**From the Message:**
> "But sin can't stop the purpose of God! God will not be defeated!"
> *--- 1958-06-19 - Ye must be born again - p44*

> "The same devil in a different form of a kingship tried to destroy God's only Son. See, getting rid of the children first, before they get a start. The devil is a real smart shrewd bird. He knows how to hit the thing before it gets started; he tries to."
> *--- 1963-06-30 - The Third Exodus - p97*

**Sub-points:**
- A. Pharaoh feared Israel's multiplication --- his suppression produced Moses, their deliverer
- B. Herod feared the Messiah's birth --- his slaughter fulfilled prophecy and could not touch the Christ
- C. Every act of oppression driven by fear has become a stepping stone in God's redemptive plan

### Person Profile: Herod (The Ruler Who Fought Against Prophecy)
- **Visible need:** To protect his throne from a rival king
- **Invisible need:** A revelation of who Christ truly was --- not an earthly king but the King of Glory
- **Motive:** Political survival masked as national security
- **Intention:** To kill the Christ child before He could rise to power
- **What God saw:** A pawn of the enemy, unknowingly fulfilling the scripture: "In Rama was there a voice heard, lamentation, and weeping, and great mourning" (Matthew 2:18)

### THUS SAITH THE LORD --- The Vision
Saints, do not be afraid of the bamboo curtain or the iron curtain or any curtain that man can hang. Brother Branham warned us plainly --- watch the purple curtain, the religious system. But even that purple curtain will not stop the purpose of God for His Bride. Every pharaoh, every Herod, every system that lifts itself against the Almighty will be ground to powder. And the Stone cut without hands will smash every kingdom of this world. Fear nothing. God cannot be defeated.

## POINT 4 --- EVERY REVIVAL PRODUCES TWINS

**Key Scripture:** "And the LORD said unto her, Two nations are in thy womb, and two manner of people shall be separated from thy bowels; and the one people shall be stronger than the other people; and the elder shall serve the younger." --- Genesis 25:23

### The Human Need (Humanistic)
- In every family, every church, every nation --- wherever God is moving, there are always two responses: one that reaches for the birthright and one that despises it.
- Jacob and Esau came from the same womb, the same holy parents. Esau was the gentleman, the worker, the one everybody would have picked. But Jacob had one thing on his mind: the birthright. He would do anything, go through anything, to possess it.
- The competition between these brothers is not just ancient history. It is happening right now --- in nations, in churches, in your own heart.

### Divine Revelation
**The Word says:**
> "As it is written, Jacob have I loved, but Esau have I hated." --- Romans 9:13

**The type unfolds:**
The competition between Jacob and Esau is the type of every spiritual conflict in history. Esau had the position, the firstborn right by natural law. But he despised it. Jacob wanted it more than food, more than comfort, more than his own reputation. And this is the picture: the natural man always looks better on the outside --- more refined, more presentable, more reasonable. But God does not look on the outward appearance. He looks on the heart. He knew what was in Jacob before the boy was born.

**From the Message:**
> "Every revival produces twins, just as Jacob and Rebekah produced twins --- Esau and Jacob. I mean Isaac instead of ... Isaac and Rebekah, instead of Jacob. Isaac and Rebekah produced twins. The father was holy, the mother was holy. But they had two boys born --- Esau and Jacob. Now, both of them was religious. But Esau, when it come to works and deeds as a good legalist, he was probably far better than Jacob."
> *--- 1960-12-05 - Church Ages, part 3: The Ephesian Church Age - p48*

> "But in his heart he wanted that birthright. He didn't care about anything else; he wanted that birthright. That's all he wanted. And Esau wanted to be a nice fellow, and take care of everything, and do everything just right... He was a real good legalist; Esau was."
> *--- 1959-12-23 - Questions and Answers - p40*

**Sub-points:**
- A. The competition is never between two equals --- it is between the natural man and the spiritual man
- B. Esau's tragedy was not that he lacked ability, but that he lacked hunger for the right thing
- C. Jacob's triumph was not in his morality but in his desperation for God's promise

### THUS SAITH THE LORD --- The Vision
Bride, you are the Jacob of this hour. The world looks at you and sees nothing impressive --- a small people, a despised message, no great buildings or television programs. But you have the birthright. You have the promise. You have the Token. And all the Esaus in the world --- with their programs, their organizations, their political might --- cannot take from you what God has sovereignly given. Do not envy Esau's table. You have the birthright. That is enough.

## POINT 5 --- NOTHING CAN DEFEAT THE PURPOSE OF GOD

**Key Scripture:** "A wise man is strong; yea, a man of knowledge increaseth strength." --- Proverbs 24:5

### The Human Need (Humanistic)
- We see the nations rising against one another. We see tariff wars, technology bans, military posturing. And the natural man asks, "Who will win?"
- But the spiritual man asks a different question: "What is God doing in the midst of all this?"
- Beloved, the same Daniel who prophesied that knowledge would increase also prophesied that "the people that do know their God shall be strong, and do exploits." There is a knowledge of the world that produces fear and sorrow. And there is a knowledge of God that produces strength and victory. The question is not how much you know. The question is: what kind of knowledge do you carry?

### Divine Revelation
**The Word says:**
> "And such as do wickedly against the covenant shall he corrupt by flatteries: but the people that do know their God shall be strong, and do exploits." --- Daniel 11:32

**The type unfolds:**
Daniel's prophecy lays two streams side by side: the increase of worldly knowledge and the increase of revelation knowledge. Both are increasing in this end-time. The world has its advanced chips, its artificial intelligence, its rare earth minerals. But the Bride has something the world cannot manufacture --- the revealed Word of God for this hour. And it is that knowledge --- not silicon, not semiconductors, but the knowledge of the living God --- that increaseth strength.

**From the Message:**
> "Daniel said, 'In that day they run to and fro, knowledge shall increase.' And he said, 'The wise shall know their God in that day, and shall do great exploits in that day. But the wicked will not know the God of heaven.' They know Him in a form and in a ritual, like our first lesson said, but they don't know Him by the way of perfection."
> *--- 1957-09-15 - Hebrews, Chapter 7, part 1 - p19*

> "Nothing can defeat the purpose of God! Nothing can. Therefore, I rest assured upon the promise, 'Because I live, ye shall live also.'"
> *--- 1958-04-06 - I know my Redeemer liveth - p30*

**Sub-points:**
- A. Worldly knowledge increases sorrow (Ecclesiastes 1:18) --- it brings power but no peace, capability but no rest
- B. Spiritual knowledge increases strength (Proverbs 24:5) --- the man who knows his God does not need to compete; he simply stands on the promise
- C. God has sealed and now unsealed this Book for the end-time Bride. The knowledge that matters most is not being fought over in trade wars --- it is being poured out from the Throne of God upon a little flock who will receive it

### THUS SAITH THE LORD --- The Vision
While the nations fight over rare earth minerals and semiconductor chips, God is pouring out the greatest wealth the world has ever known --- the revelation of the Seven Seals, the mystery of the Thunders, the full knowledge of Jesus Christ for the end-time Bride. Saints, you possess something that no tariff can restrict, no embargo can block, and no government can ban. You have the Word of God made manifest. And that knowledge --- that divine, revealed, sovereign knowledge --- is what will carry the Bride from this world into Glory. Let the nations rage. The purpose of God stands sure.

## CONCLUSION

### The Epiphany --- The Question Answered

> Can you stop the purpose of God? When a man, a nation, or an empire shifts
> from pursuing excellence to hindering others from rising --- what does God
> say about that, and what does He do with it?
>
> "No man, no nation, no devil in hell can stop the purpose of Almighty God.
> Every attempt to hinder what God has ordained becomes a tool in His hand
> to accomplish the very thing the enemy tried to prevent. The Bride's only
> business is to know her God, carry His Word, and rest in the certainty
> that He who began a good work will perform it until the day of Jesus Christ."

### Summary

- **Point 1:** Civilization travels with the sun by divine decree --- no nation can redirect what God has set in motion.
- **Point 2:** The deadliest shift in a man's heart is when he moves from building himself up to tearing others down --- this is the spirit of Cain.
- **Point 3:** Every act of fear-driven destruction --- from Pharaoh's slaughter to Herod's massacre --- has only served to advance God's purpose.
- **Point 4:** Every revival produces twins --- the natural and the spiritual --- and the birthright always belongs to the one who hungers for it.
- **Point 5:** The knowledge that matters is not worldly power but the revealed Word of God, which alone increaseth true strength.

> **The heart of this message:** You cannot stop the sun from moving, and you cannot stop the purpose of God. Stop competing. Start resting. The Bride has a birthright that no empire on earth can take away.

### A Closing Hymn

**Suggested hymn:** "My Faith Looks Up to Thee"
**Why:** This hymn turns the eyes away from the fear and competition of the world and lifts them to the Lamb of God --- the only source of strength, the only object of true faith, the only One who holds the purpose that cannot be defeated.

Part 2 --- "The Purple Curtain" (the real threat is not political but religious --- Rome). Part 3 --- "The Knowledge That Increaseth Strength" (the Bride's inheritance versus the world's technology).
